What It Is

The missing layer between procurement and maintenance.

ApartmentCart is a software platform that sits between the people who negotiate pricing and the people who buy things. It gives procurement a way to publish negotiated prices, gives maintenance a way to find and order the right parts, and gives owners real-time visibility into whether the savings are actually landing.

How It Works

Three steps.
Zero leakage.

ApartmentCart replaces the broken handoff between procurement and maintenance with a digital workflow that ensures every purchase follows the plan.

01
Step 1
Build your catalog
Procurement teams digitize property plan sets and create per-unit asset catalogs. Link negotiated vendor pricing to specific SKUs. Every property gets a living, searchable catalog of what's installed.
02
Step 2
Equip the field
When a maintenance tech gets a work order, they open ApartmentCart, see exactly what's installed at that unit — faucet model, flooring SKU, appliance type — and order the right part at the pre-negotiated price. No binders. No guessing. No Home Depot runs.
03
Step 3
See the savings
Owners and operators get real-time visibility into procurement compliance. See what's being bought, whether it's on-contract, and how much is being saved vs. leaked. The P&L finally tells the truth.
